Positive Association: Green is of nature, of balance and growth. It is restful and secure, symbolizing harmony, healing, and stability. It also represents security and self-reliance. Darker greens relate to money, wealth and prestige, while lighter greens relate to rebirth, growth, and freshness.

Negative Assosiation: Too much green can lead to feelings of envy, greed, jealousy, and selfishness.
