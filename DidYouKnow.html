While perceptions of color are some what subjective colors can have universal effects and/or meaning. Colors (red, orange, and yellow) in the red area of the color spectrum are known as warm colors. These warm colors evoke emotions ranging from feelings of warmth and comfort to feelings of anger and hostility. Colors (blue, purple, and green) on the blue side of the spectrum are known as cool colors. These colors are often described as calm, but can also call to mind feelings of sadness or indifference.
